Team Outsourcing

We build IT teams. In a relatively short period of time we are able to initiate a recruitment process, form a team consisting of our own IT specialists, and direct the team’s development to a required number of members (also through recruitment activities).

We offer the services of our program manager, who gets to be responsible for the team’s work and plays the role of a intermediary between the team and the client. We have developed certain standards, which help us to successfully determine the scope of responsibilities and to build an effective client-team communication channel.

If we are to deal with a brand new project, the project management methodology can be tailored to our clients’ needs and requirements.

When we have an already partially developed product on hand, we know how to transfer necessary knowledge.

We know how to motivate people in our teams and how to maintain team spirit.

Feel free to arrange a meeting with us; we will be glad to show how can we be of help.
