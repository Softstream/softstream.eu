Brewery Marketing Activities

Customer

One of the largest American-owned breweries.

Objective and solution

The application had to be utilized to place orders for promotional items. Orders may be placed by authorized associates via friendly web-based user interface. The application implies the back-end which is utilized by the application administrators to manage orders, users and other specific information.

Solution:

  • Application was built on top of ASP.NET
  • Integrated with online purchase service to make it possible to process payment in various ways
  • Employed Microsoft Reporting Services to generate sales reports

Project highlights

  • Development boosted by 3rd party components library
  • Successfully ported rich UI experience onto tablet devices
  • Customer saved resources that were spent on marketing activities
